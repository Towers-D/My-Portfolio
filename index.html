About Me

By Charley Pearce

I am a programmer from the North East of England. I have completed a BSc with honours in Computer Science at Newcastle University and am currently continuing studying there on an MSc in Computer Games Engineering.

I have enjoyed playing video games since I was a child, this interest along with an interest in I.T. made me want to become a Game Programmer. I have enjoyed, as part of my time at university, creating games. I find Games development, as well as programming in general, a challenging, yet rewarding endeavour. I enjoy figuring out the solutions to the problems that are presented to me to implement a feature.
